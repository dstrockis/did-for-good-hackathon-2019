Simple, secure access to non-profit provider services online

contoso.gov offers the public secure and private online access to participating non-profit provider programs. With one contoso.gov account, users can sign in and redeem multiple services. Our goal is to make managing benefits, services and applications easier and more secure.

Simple and secure for the public

Because contoso.gov is a shared service, users need fewer passwords and learn fewer interfaces. Also, security experts protect one service instead of many. Dedicated teams of design and security experts also will continuously improve it.

Saving time and money

Contoso.gov handles software development, security operations, and customer support. This frees up non-profit organizations to focus on their missions while reducing costs and improving security.

Built cooperatively

Contoso.gov works with the private sector and nonprofits to identify and implement best practices and new standards.
